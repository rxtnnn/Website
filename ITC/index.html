Best time to go to Cebu

Sirao Flower Garden
| January to April

If you’re planning to go to Cebu to do water activities, the best time to go would be from January to April. The weather during these months is ideal because there are fewer chances of rain and it’s not too humid as well.

Dry and Summer Season
| May to December

These months are the ideal time to visit Cebu for warm-weather activities such as swimming, diving, canyoneering, and sightseeing. With less rainfall, you can enjoy the outdoor activities and water adventures fully.

Sinulog Festival | January
